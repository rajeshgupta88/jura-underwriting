NOTICE REGARDING CREDIT-BASED INSURANCE SCORING
Example Mutual Insurance Co

NOTICE: Example Mutual Insurance Co has used credit-based insurance scoring in
determining your premium. You have the right to request a re-evaluation of your
premium if you believe your credit information is inaccurate or if you have
experienced a life event (such as divorce, illness, or identity theft) that may
have adversely affected your credit history.

To request a re-evaluation, contact your agent or Example Mutual Insurance Co
directly. We will review your request and notify you of any resulting change to
your premium within 30 days.

For questions about your rights under California Insurance Code §1861.05,
contact the California Department of Insurance at 1-800-927-4357.
