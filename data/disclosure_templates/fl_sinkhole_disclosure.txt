SINKHOLE COVERAGE NOTICE: Florida law requires disclosure that your commercial
property policy includes coverage for catastrophic ground cover collapse as
defined under Florida Statutes §627.706. This is not the same as comprehensive
sinkhole coverage.

Catastrophic ground cover collapse coverage applies only when all four of the
following conditions occur simultaneously:
  1. Abrupt collapse of the ground cover
  2. A depression in the ground cover clearly visible to the naked eye
  3. Structural damage to the covered building, including the foundation
  4. The insured structure is condemned and ordered to be vacated by the
     governmental agency authorized to issue such an order

Optional sinkhole loss coverage, as defined under Florida Statutes §627.7063,
may be available for an additional premium. Please contact your agent for
details.

For questions about sinkhole coverage requirements, contact the Florida
Department of Financial Services at 1-877-693-5236.
